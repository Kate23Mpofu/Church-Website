About Us

Learn about out mission, vision, beliefs, and the people who make up Mughodi Trust Church.

Our Mission

To spread the love of Christ through worship, fellowship, and service, transforming lives and strengthening communities through God's word.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and love in our community, raising disciples who impact the world for Christ's kingdom.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the Trinity, salvation through Christ, the authority of Scripture, and the power of prayer to transform lives.

Our Story

A Legacy of Faith

Mughodi Trust Church was founded in 2008 by Pastor John and Grace Mughodi with a simple but powerful vision: to create a place where people could experience God's love and grow in their faith journey.

Starting with just 12 members meeting in a small rented hall, our church has grown to serve over 500 families across the region. We've witnessed countless testimonies of transformation, healing, and spiritual growth.

In 2015, we moved to our current location and built our beautiful sanctuary, which now serves as a hub for worship, community events, and outreach programs. Our church has become a cornerstone of the community, providing not just spiritual guidance but also practical support through various ministries.

Today, we continue to grow and expand our impact, with plans for new ministries, community programs, and outreach initiatives that will further our mission of spreading Christ's love.
